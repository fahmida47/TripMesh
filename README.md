# TripMesh
TripMesh is an online tourism platform that connects tourists, guide service companies, and the website administrator. Tourists can create accounts, explore different guide service company profiles, view service details, prices, and ratings, and filter services based on price and rating. They can send booking requests to their preferred guide service companies and make payments after the requests are accepted. Guide service companies can create profiles, provide service information, receive booking requests, and accept or reject them. After accepting a request, the company provides the guide service to the tourist. The administrator manages tourist and guide company accounts, monitors booking requests, and verifies guide service companies. The administrator also receives and holds the tourist’s payment securely until the trip is completed. After the trip, the administrator deducts the TripMesh commission and transfers the remaining amount to the guide service company.  tourists can rate and review the guide service based on their experience.
